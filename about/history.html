Our History

The Museum of Asian Art & Art Gallery in Malaysia is an institution dedicated to celebrating the rich and diverse artistic traditions of Asia. As one of the key cultural landmarks in Malaysia, its history reflects the country's evolving relationship with art, culture, and heritage, with a focus on the Asian continent's artistic legacy.

The Museum of Asian Art & Art Gallery in Malaysia was established in the early 1990s with the mission to showcase the diverse artistic traditions of Asia. The museum was founded to preserve and promote the rich cultural heritage of the region, featuring both traditional and contemporary works from various Asian civilizations, including Chinese, Indian, Southeast Asian, and Islamic art.Over the years, the museum has grown into an important cultural institution in Malaysia, with its Art Gallery component providing a dynamic space for contemporary Asian artists to showcase their work. The museum has hosted numerous significant exhibitions, fostering cultural exchange and understanding across Asia.
