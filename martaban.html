Martaban of Peace / Penalty

Description:
'Head and hand' jar, with plain shoulder, eight vertical ear-loops, glazed brown, the hand vertically applied.
Another version, hand in horizontally position. Guangzhou, Qing Dynasty, second half 18th century.
This collection was acquired from Sarawak. Purchased from Sotheby's, London, 12/10/1981
