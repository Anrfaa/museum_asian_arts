Glazed Porcelain Elephant Image From Sukhothai, Thailand

Description:
An image of the elephant and the herdsman was created as talisman for the village.The process of creating it is
a complicated one where each animal shape is created individually and then layered with brown-colored slip.
Regarded as holy by the Siamese community, the images of the elephant is created with great care. This image is
also believed to be on the urn for a highly respected person or phrya dhatu.
