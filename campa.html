Champa, Stoneware Jar

Description:
Champa of Vietnam jar with four horizontal loop handles. Decorated with medallions of flowers. Shipwreck finding.
Horizontal loop handles, statues like design in between medallions. Most of the Champa jars have a brown glaze, some
with polychrome colour, they are light and not heavily potted. Circa 16th century, Champa, Vietnam.
